Welcome to Golden Blades

Located in the serene environment of Kilimani at Menelik Suites, Golden Blades Barbershop offers a premium grooming experience with ample parking and a relaxing atmosphere.

Our friendly professional staff are trained to ensure each client leaves with a smile. Whether you need a haircut, beard trim, or full spa treatment, we provide exceptional service with attention to detail.

Feeling tired or stressed? Golden Blades is the perfect place to unwind and get pampered with our range of services including facials, manicures, pedicures, and massage treatments.
